Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I get the products after purchase?

+

After completing your purchase, you'll receive instant access to download your files in your Cfx account. Each product comes with detailed installation instructions. Here's the general process:

  • Download the product files from your Cfx assets portal
  • Extract the archive to your server's resources folder
  • Add the resource to your server.cfg file
  • Restart your server or run the refresh command
  • Start the resource using "ensure [resource-name]"

If you encounter any issues, our support team is available 24/7 on Discord to help you through the installation process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • PayPal: Secure and widely trusted
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
  • Cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum (coming soon)
  • Discord Nitro: Select regions only

All transactions are processed securely through our payment partners. You'll receive your files immediately after payment confirmation.

What is your refund policy?

+

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • 24-hour refund window: Request a refund within a day of purchase
  • Valid reasons: Product doesn't work as described, major bugs not disclosed
  • Digital products: Due to the nature of digital products, refunds are evaluated case-by-case

To request a refund, open a support ticket on our Discord server with your order details and reason for the refund. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours.

What frameworks do your scripts support?

+

Our scripts support the most popular FiveM frameworks:

  • QB-Core: Full compatibility with latest version
  • ESX: Supported on selected scripts (check product page)
  • QBOX: Many scripts work with QBOX frameworks
  • Standalone: Some scripts are framework-agnostic

Each product page clearly indicates which frameworks are supported. If you're unsure, feel free to ask our support team before purchasing.

Do I get free updates after purchase?

+

Yes! All our products come with free lifetime updates. When we release improvements, bug fixes, or new features:

  • You'll be notified via Discord
  • Updated files will be available in your account portal
  • Update instructions are included with each release
  • Major updates include migration guides if needed

We continuously improve our products based on customer feedback and FiveM platform updates.

Are your assets optimized for server performance?

+

Absolutely! Optimization is one of our core principles:

  • MLOs: Optimized LODs, proper occlusion, minimal draw calls
  • Vehicles: Reasonable poly counts, optimized textures, custom handling
  • Scripts: Zero resmon impact when idle, efficient code structure
  • Clothing: Optimized textures, proper streaming

Every product is tested on live servers before release to ensure it meets our performance standards. We typically aim for less than 0.01ms resmon for scripts.

How do I get support for my purchase?

+

Our support channels:

  • Discord (Recommended): Join our server and open a support ticket

When opening a ticket, please include:

What is your support response time?

+

We pride ourselves on fast, helpful support:

Can I request custom modifications to products?

+

Yes! We offer custom modification services for an additional fee:
